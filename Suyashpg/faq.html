FAQ

  • Who is a Paying guest ?

    A person who lives in someone else’s house and pays for food and accommodation is a paying guest, also called as lodger. Usually the stay is for short period and suitable for students, frequent travellers and working professionals as it provides inexpensive lodging including food.

  • What are the amenities provided by the PG?

    We provide food inclusive of the rent. We prepare authentic North Indian and South Indian food and also serve tea in the morning and in the evening. Every room has a 32” TV, refrigerator, bed with spring fit mattress pillow and bedspread. Everyone has access to high speed internet. Washing machine is also available in the common utility area. Some other facilities are parking space, lift, 24/7 Security, CCTV surveillance, Power Backup.

  • How many facilities does Suyash PG has?

    There are three facilities of Suyash PG in Bengaluru. One is at Marathahalli called as Marvel. Kundalahalli PG is known as Maple and the third one is Melange Suyash PG at MG Road.

  • What are the benefits of staying with us ?

    Location benefit is the most important. Our facilities are located at prime centres of Bengaluru. Marathahalli and Kundalahalli cover most of the important tech parks. Also it is very easy to find shopping centres. But the essential thing is the conveyance time is drastically reduced.

  • What is the security deposit for renting?

    Only one month security deposit is required to avail our service.
