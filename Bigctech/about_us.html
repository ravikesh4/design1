Big C Technologies Pvt. Ltd.

Founded in June 2008, Big C Technologies started with a dream of taking technology closer to people to make a difference in their daily life experience.

Over the years BCTPL has emerged as a leading specialist provider of end-to-end quality solutions in the area of IT Hardware, information security & analytics to customers across India through its Products, Services, Training and Consultancy.The company stepped up development efforts in data management, event stream processing, data visualization, analytics and in-memory distributed processing to create a modern analytics infrastructure.

Today Big C Technologies is an ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 27001:2013 certified, high-end IT consultancy company, providing strategy, technical support, innovation, and development expertise. The company delivers a variety of cloud computing services from IT Hardware to software development across the range of cloud technologies including Microsoft, VMWare, Web Apps and various online services.

Our Goal

Our goal today is to help business to rationalise, improve and run their IT systems. We form IT Strategy, centralise and standardise. We plan and manage the execution of IT projects & ensure everything keeps running for our customers range from small to big enterprise, government, and multinationals.

Our Purpose

Our core business is providing professional IT consulting services – network, desktop, server, database, web, and Internet. The backbone of our company is our team of IT specialists – senior engineers, technology Guru’s, web and Internet experts.

We foster personal, direct and transparent relationships with every client believing this is the only way to successfully conduct business.We drastically reduce costs and improve IT service for our clients by centralizing and simplifying IT systems.

Our Team

The backbone of our company is our core team of IT Guru’s and technical specialists.We are passionate, hard-working, and diverse individuals who are really good at what we do. As dedicated problem solvers, we work around the clock to achieve innovative and quality results for our clients.
